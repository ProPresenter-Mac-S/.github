## What Is ProPresenter for Mac

<a href="#">ProPresenter Mac</a> is a purpose-built live presentation application developed by
Renewed Vision, used primarily by churches, houses of worship, conference centers, corporate
events, sports venues, and broadcast productions for displaying text, images, video, and lower
thirds on multiple screens and video outputs simultaneously during live events.

<a href="#">ProPresenter Mac</a> is the standard presentation platform in modern church
production — the application behind the song lyrics on screens in thousands of churches,
the lower thirds graphics on live-streamed worship services, and the confidence monitors
showing song lyrics to worship leaders. Beyond the church context, ProPresenter is used at
corporate events, music festivals, conferences, and in broadcast environments for real-time
graphic insertion.

The application's defining characteristic is its operator workflow for live events — a
presentation operator at a computer controls what appears on the main displays in real time,
with the ability to advance through songs, jump to specific slides, trigger videos, and switch
between different presentations instantly without the delays or preparation that general-purpose
presentation tools like PowerPoint or Keynote require.

## Slide Library and Song Management

<a href="#">ProPresenter Mac</a> song and presentation library is the organizational foundation
of its church and event workflow:

### Song Library

The song library stores all worship songs, anthems, and lyric-based presentations as individual
entries. Each song has:

**Arrangement structure**: Songs are organized into sections (Verse 1, Chorus, Bridge, Outro)
that are arranged in a specific order for each presentation. Multiple arrangements of the same
song can be saved — the arrangement used on Sunday mornings may differ from the arrangement
used at evening services.

**Slide-level formatting**: Each slide's text formatting, font, size, alignment, and shadow
settings are stored with the slide. Changes to one slide do not affect others in the song.

**Background assignment**: Each song or individual slide can have a background assigned —
a solid color, a gradient, an image, or a video loop. Backgrounds display behind the text on
the output screens.

**CCLI integration**: <a href="#">ProPresenter Mac</a> integrates with CCLI SongSelect for
importing songs with accurate lyrics and tracking the CCLI usage data required for license
compliance.

### Scripture Display

<a href="#">ProPresenter Mac</a> includes built-in Bible databases in multiple translations.
Scripture is searched by reference or keyword, and selected verses are presented with automatic
text formatting:

**Reference display**: The translation, book, chapter, and verse reference appear with the
verse text — configured in the scripture slide template.

**Multi-verse passages**: Passages spanning multiple verses are automatically split across
slides with reference continuation formatting.

**Translation switching**: The operator can change Bible translations in real time with a
single selection without recreating the slide set.

## Multiple Screen Outputs

<a href="#">ProPresenter Mac</a> multi-output system is the core of its production capability:

### Output Destinations

**Main/House screens**: The primary audience-facing display showing lyrics, scripture, and
presentation content. This is what the congregation or audience sees.

**Stage display**: A confidence monitor visible to worship leaders and musicians facing the
stage. The stage display typically shows the current lyric, the next lyric, a clock, and
MIDI or click track information. The stage display is configured independently from the
house display.

**Announcement loop**: A separate output channel for pre-service and between-service
announcement slides that run independently of the main presentation.

**Confidence monitor**: An additional display for the presentation operator showing the
current slide, next slide, and presentation controls.

### Video Output Configuration

<a href="#">ProPresenter Mac</a> sends output to connected display hardware:

**HDMI and DisplayPort**: Direct connection to projectors, LED walls, and displays through
the Mac's video outputs. A Mac with two video outputs (or with a Thunderbolt docking station
adding outputs) handles main house plus stage display from a single Mac.

**Blackmagic DeckLink**: Professional broadcast-grade SDI output through Blackmagic Design
capture and output cards for integration with broadcast switchers and recording systems.

**NDI (ProPresenter 7)**: Network video output over Ethernet for integration with network-based
video production systems — Tricaster, NewTek, OBS, and other NDI-compatible production systems.

### Motion Graphics Templates
